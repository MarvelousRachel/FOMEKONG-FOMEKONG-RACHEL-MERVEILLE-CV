Advancements in Sensor Fusion for Underwater SLAM: A Review on Enhanced Navigation and Environmental Perception

Fomekong, F. F., Jia, B., Xu, Z., & Fred, B. (2024)

Sensors, 24(23), 7490

This comprehensive review examines recent advancements in sensor fusion techniques for underwater SLAM systems, focusing on how multiple sensor inputs can be integrated to enhance navigation accuracy and environmental perception in challenging underwater conditions.

The paper analyzes various sensor fusion approaches, including visual-inertial systems, LiDAR-camera combinations, and multi-modal data integration frameworks. Special attention is given to addressing the unique challenges of underwater environments, such as limited visibility, refraction effects, and dynamic scenes. The review also discusses emerging trends in deep learning-based sensor fusion and their potential applications in underwater robotics.

Enhancing Underwater SLAM Navigation and Perception: A Comprehensive Review of Deep Learning Integration

Fomekong, F. F., Jia, B., Xu, Z., & Fred, B. (2024)

Sensors, 24(21), 7034

This review explores how deep learning techniques are revolutionizing underwater SLAM systems, improving feature extraction, object detection, and scene understanding in challenging underwater environments.

The paper provides a systematic review of deep learning architectures applied to underwater navigation problems, including CNNs for feature extraction, RNNs for trajectory prediction, and specialized networks for dealing with water turbidity and lighting variations. The review highlights recent breakthroughs in end-to-end SLAM systems and discusses the practical challenges of implementing these solutions on resource-constrained underwater platforms.

The Influence of Industry 4.0: A Case Study of Injection Molding Machines, Drones, 3D Printing, and Automation Factories

Fomekong, F. F., & Hugejile. (2021)

North American Academic Research, 4(12), 221-238

This research examines the impact of Industry 4.0 technologies on manufacturing processes, with specific focus on injection molding, drone applications, 3D printing advancements, and factory automation.

The paper presents case studies from various manufacturing sectors, analyzing how digital transformation is reshaping traditional production methods. The research highlights the integration of IoT sensors in injection molding machines, the use of drones for quality control and monitoring, the role of additive manufacturing in rapid prototyping, and the implementation of automation systems in modern factories. The findings demonstrate significant improvements in efficiency, quality, and flexibility across the studied applications.
